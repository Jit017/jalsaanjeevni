Drip Irrigation

An efficient method of watering crops by directly targeting plant roots.

Rainwater Harvesting

Greywater Recycling

Trending Articles

Fog Harvesting

Collects water from fog using specialized nets, providing a sustainable source in arid regions.

Atmospheric Water Generators (AWG)

Extracts moisture from the air and condenses it into drinkable water.

Hydrogel Agriculture

Hydrogels absorb water and release it slowly to plant roots, enhancing water efficiency in agriculture.

Cloud Seeding

A weather modification technique where substances like silver iodide are dispersed into clouds to encourage rainfall.
